TPS Story

Jordan Mattox set out to raise the standard of lawncare by focusing on dependability, professionalism, and eco-conscious practices. From humble beginnings serving local neighborhoods, TPS has grown into a trusted partner for homeowners, HOAs, and commercial clients across Georgia.

Our core members saw a need for a company that not only beautifies outdoor spaces but also values long-term client relationships, fair pricing, and eco-friendly practices. Today, TPS continues to grow while staying true to its commitment of delivering quality with integrity.

Mission, Values & Vision

Our Mission

To elevate outdoor care by providing reliable, professional, and eco-conscious services that enhance the beauty, safety, and long-term value of every property we touch.

Our Values

  • Trust: Standing behind every project with integrity, honesty, and accountability.
  • Professionalism: Delivering precision, respect, and consistency across all residential, commercial, and gov't services.
  • Sustainability: Using environmentally responsible practices that protect people, properties, and the planet.
  • Innovation: Adapting modern tools, techniques, and strategies to deliver smarter and more efficient results.
  • Community: Building lasting partnerships and investing in local growth by creating opportunities and fostering pride in every neighborhood we serve.

Our Vision

To become Georgia’s premier property care partner, expanding heavily into the government sectors while remaining rooted in quality, care, and sustainability. We aim to set the standard in lawn care and facility maintenance; where every detail matters, and every project builds a legacy.

Founder Foundation | Lawn Boys of ATL

The journey began with a youthful lawn service that taught perseverance, teamwork, and the pride of creating something meaningful outdoors. What started with neighborhood yards became the roots of today’s vision; a company built on persistence, detail, and care.

Years of trial, growth, and resilience shaped Top Performance Services into what it is today: a business with proven operational success, strong industry connections, and a dedicated team capable of managing both residential and commercial properties. Some of the earliest teammates still contribute, carrying forward history and commitment.

As TPS looks ahead, the focus is on sustainable growth, new opportunities, and building lasting partnerships. Every service reflects the belief that small efforts create lasting impact and that true quality is found in the details.
